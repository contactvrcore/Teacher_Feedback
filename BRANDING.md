# VRCORE Education Branding Guidelines

Welcome to the official branding guidelines for **VRCORE Education**. This document serves as the definitive source for our visual identity, ensuring consistency, professionalism, and recognition across all platforms and communications.

## 1. Brand Identity

**Mission:** To revolutionize education through immersive virtual reality experiences that engage students and empower educators.

**Tone of Voice:**
*   **Enthusiastic:** We are passionate about the future of learning.
*   **Informative:** We provide clear, valuable insights into VR technology.
*   **Accessible:** We speak to everyone—educators, students, and parents—avoiding unnecessary jargon.
*   **Innovative:** Our language reflects our cutting-edge approach.

---

## 2. Color Palette

Our color palette reflects trust, technology, and clarity. Use these codes to maintain color consistency across digital and print media.

### Primary Colors
These are the dominant colors of the brand, used for primary actions, headers, and key brand elements.

| Color Name | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **VRCORE Blue** | `#1A73E8` | `rgb(26, 115, 232)` | Primary buttons, links, active states, key icons. |
| **VRCORE Dark Blue** | `#0D47A1` | `rgb(13, 71, 161)` | Navigation bars, footers, deep contrast backgrounds. |

### Secondary Colors
These colors support the primary palette, providing background and accent options.

| Color Name | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **VRCORE Light Blue** | `#E3F2FD` | `rgb(227, 242, 253)` | Hover states, light backgrounds, subtle accents. |
| **VRCORE Gray** | `#F5F5F5` | `rgb(245, 245, 245)` | Page backgrounds, cards, section dividers. |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | `rgb(255, 255, 255)` | Content backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds. |

## 3. Typography

Typography is a crucial part of our visual language. We prioritize readability and a modern, clean aesthetic.

### Primary Font: **Roboto**
Used for headings, titles, and calls to action. It conveys a modern and geometric feel.
*   **Weights:** Bold (700), Medium (500)
*   *Fallback:* Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif

### Secondary Font: **Open Sans**
Used for body text, descriptions, and long-form content. It is friendly and highly legible.
*   **Weights:** Regular (400), Semi-Bold (600)
*   *Fallback:* Segoe UI, Tahoma, sans-serif

### Hierarchy Example
*   **Heading 1 (H1):** Roboto Bold, 32px - 48px
*   **Heading 2 (H2):** Roboto Medium, 24px - 32px
*   **Body Text (p):** Open Sans Regular, 16px - 18px

---

## 4. Logo Usage

The VRCORE Education logo is our most recognizable asset. Treat it with respect.

*   **Clear Space:** Maintain a clear margin around the logo roughly equivalent to the height of the "V" icon. No other elements should intrude into this space.
*   **Minimum Size:**
    *   **Digital:** 150px width.
    *   **Print:** 1 inch width.
*   **Do's:**
    *   Use the logo on white or light gray backgrounds.
    *   Use the white version of the logo on dark blue backgrounds.
*   **Don'ts:**
    *   Do not stretch or distort the logo.
    *   Do not change the logo colors.
    *   Do not place the logo on busy, high-contrast images that reduce legibility.

---

## 5. UI Components

Consistent UI elements ensure a seamless user experience.

*   **Buttons:**
    *   *Primary:* `VRCORE Blue` background, White text, rounded corners.
    *   *Hover:* darken slightly or shift to `VRCORE Dark Blue`.
    *   *Secondary:* Transparent background with `VRCORE Blue` border and text.
*   **Navigation Bar:**
    *   Background: `VRCORE Dark Blue`.
    *   Text: White.
    *   Active Item: Underlined or highlighted in `VRCORE Light Blue`.
*   **Links:**
    *   Standard text links should be `VRCORE Blue`.
    *   Hover effects should add an underline or change background color to `VRCORE Light Blue`.

---

## 6. Imagery & Iconography

*   **Imagery:**
    *   Focus on **active engagement**: Students wearing VR headsets, teachers guiding lessons, bright classrooms.
    *   Style: High quality, well-lit, candid (avoid overly staged stock photos), diverse representation.
*   **Iconography:**
    *   Style: Clean, line-based icons (outline style).
    *   Color: `VRCORE Blue` or `VRCORE Dark Blue`.
    *   Stroke Width: Consistent medium weight (e.g., 2px).
